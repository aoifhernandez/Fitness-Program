WHY WORK WITH ME?

After over 20 years of soccer, I struggled to maintain training habits without the sports I loved. Without a clear purpose, I found it very difficult to stick to a strict training routine whcih I had loved as an athlete. To fill that gap, I decided to take on new challenges such as weight lifting, biking, swimming and marathons to help me regain meaning and motivation for my training routine. I will continue to drive to pursue more challenges because chasing after a goal pushes me to maintain a disciplined lifestyle, physical fitness, and diet which all contributes to improving my overall health and wellbeing. My approach is to support everyone in building sustainable habits taking actions consistentently, and holding accoutability through my interactive program to achieve goals together.
