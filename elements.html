WHY WORK WITH ME?

Left & Right

Over the course of my athletic career, I have developed the most optimal routine to maximize my fitness level and achieve my goals. Some of my accomplishments include playing sports at the collegiate level, running full marathons, competing in a half-Ironman relay race, and more. Achieving fitness goals exemplifies the concept of delayed gratification, where consistent effort over a long period leads to success. Practicing disciplined habits to reach these goals has given me confidence and self-respect that I wouldn't have otherwise gained. My approach is to support everyone in building sustainable habits(exercising, dieting, and sleeping), consistent effort, and accoutability through my interactive program to achieve our goal together.
